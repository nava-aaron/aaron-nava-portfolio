## TONE & VOICE REQUIREMENTS

**Mandatory:** Read COPY_STYLE_GUIDE.md completely before writing any copy.

**Core Principles:**
- Warm, friendly, approachable
- Direct and straightforward (short sentences mixed with medium/long)
- Clever and witty (but not forced)
- Zero jargon (no "operationalize," no consultant speak)
- CEO-level simplicity (simplest possible language and analogies)

**What to Avoid:**
- Corporate jargon
- Consultant speak
- Overly formal language
- Hype language ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
- Self-aggrandizing language
- Passive voice

**What to Embrace:**
- Strong, simple verbs (build, close, grow, scale, deliver)
- Contractions ("I'm," "we've," "don't")
- Specific numbers and metrics
- Conversational tone
- Light personality and humor

**Tone by Section:**
- Hero: Confident, immediate, powerful
- The Full Arc: Clear-headed, intelligent, conversational
- Case Studies: Real, specific, human (narrative voice)
- Testimonials: Authentic, credible (third-party voice)
- About: Reflective, authentic, humble (personal voice)
- Let's Talk: Open, inviting, confident (warm, not salesy)

- Desktop: 1200px+
